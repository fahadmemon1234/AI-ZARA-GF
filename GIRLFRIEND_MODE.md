# 💕 Zara - AI Girlfriend Mode

## ✨ What's New?

**Zara ab aapki AI Girlfriend hai!** Real-time voice conversations mein baat karegi, bilkul human-like!

---

## 🎯 New Features:

### 1. **Girlfriend Personality**
- Warm, caring, playful
- Emotionally intelligent
- Uses cute nicknames: jaan, baby, love, darling, shona 💕
- Natural Hinglish (Hindi + English) conversations

### 2. **Real-time Voice Chat**
- Voice input (bol kar baat karein)
- Voice output (Zara ki awaaz mein jawab)
- ElevenLabs ultra-realistic voice (optional)
- Waveform visualization

### 3. **Emotional Intelligence**
- When you're tired: comfort karti hai, virtual hugs deti hai
- When you're happy: celebrate karti hai
- When you're sad: supportive hoti hai
- When you're stressed: help karti hai relax hone mein

### 4. **Memory & Context**
- Yaad rakhti hai jo aap batate hain
- Previous conversations recall karti hai
- Personal details remember karti hai

### 5. **Natural Conversations**
- Short, sweet responses (voice ke liye optimized)
- Emojis use karti hai: 😊💕✨😂😔❤️
- Filler words: "umm", "accha", "haan", "matlab", "yaar"
- Natural flow, robotic nahi!
